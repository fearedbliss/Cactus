## Installation

- Extract the platform into your **`Platforms`** Directory.
- Add an entry to Cactus called **`Blackened (3.5 Beta)`** that launches
  **`Game.exe`**.

  **`Blackened.mpq`**, deleted the **`PlugY`** files, and re-packed the rest of
